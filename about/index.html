Short Biosketch

I study at the University of Maryland, where I'm a member of Team TOXIC in the Gemstone Honors College and the QUEST Honors Program. I also work as a research assistant in the Gutierrez Group, where I run scripts in Gaussian to better understand organic mechanisms. Outside the lab & classroom, my primary involvements are as Parliamentarian for the Student Government Association and the Public Relations Chair for Omicron Delta Kappa, the leading honors society on campus.
