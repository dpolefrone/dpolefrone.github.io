Short Biosketch

I study at the University of Maryland, where I'm a member of Team TOXIC in the Gemstone Honors College as well as the QUEST Honors Program. I also work as a research assistant in the Gutierrez Group, where I run scripts in Gaussian to better understand how nickel can form carbon-carbon bonds. Outside the lab & classroom, my primary involvements are as Chief of Staff for the Student Government Association and the Executive Board of Omicron Delta Kappa, the leading honors society on campus. Recently, I've been working as a Data Fellow with Bluebonnet Data, supporting down-ballot progressive causes.
