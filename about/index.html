Short Biosketch

I study at the University of Pennsylvania, where I'm a first-year graduate student in the Kozlowski group in the Department of Chemistry at the University of Pennsylvania.

Previously, I studied at the University of Maryland, where I worked on cheminformatics with Team TOXIC in the Gemstone Honors College as well as the QUEST Honors Program. I also worked as a research assistant in the Gutierrez Group, where I ran scripts in Gaussian to better understand how nickel can form carbon-carbon bonds. Outside the lab & classroom, I was active in student government and Omicron Delta Kappa, the leading honors society on campus. I also have worked with Bluebonnet Data, in support of down-ballot progressive causes.
